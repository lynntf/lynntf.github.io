Alpha, Beta, and Gamma are all angles. They can take any value you want, but all possible behaviors are represented by angles between 0 degrees and 180 degrees.

This is an implementation of the bi-axial hemispherical piecewise isometry (PWI) that I study in my research. See my paper on this PWI or my paper on getting meaning from the coloring for more detail.

You can find this sketch on the p5.js website as well to play around with it.
